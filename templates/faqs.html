Most Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Master Error to debug your code.

What programming languages does Master Error support?

Master Error currently supports Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, and HTML/CSS. We're continuously adding support for more languages. Our AI-powered analysis can often provide helpful insights even for languages not fully supported yet.

How accurate are the error detections?

Our AI achieves approximately 92% accuracy in error detection for supported languages. For complex or ambiguous cases, we provide multiple potential solutions with confidence ratings. We recommend reviewing all suggestions and testing them in your environment.

Can Master Error help me learn programming concepts?

Absolutely! Master Error is designed not just to fix errors but to help you understand them. Each solution includes explanations of:

1. What caused the error
2. Why our solution works
3. How to avoid similar issues in the future
4. Related programming concepts to explore

Is my code stored or analyzed for other purposes?

No, we prioritize your privacy. Code processed through Master Error is used solely for generating your immediate debugging solutions and is not stored or used for any other purposes. We don't collect or analyze your code for any secondary uses.
